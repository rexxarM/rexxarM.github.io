Miehanav:Lord

Welcome back to the Mirhana Continent.

Here, you will step into the role of a lord. The prosperity of agriculture, the circulation of commerce, the rise of handicrafts, even the kingdom’s tax revenues and the stability of the people’s hearts—the balance of it all rests on your every thought.
Within a limited ruling cycle, each of your edict deployments will either steer your domain toward everlasting prosperity or plunge it into an abyss of eternal ruin.
Facing the immense pressure of resources and the onslaught of unexpected events, are you ready to take on the challenge?

You must continuously make choices under resource pressure and relentless unexpected events:

Edict Operations:

Arrange the execution order among limited options, balancing immediate gains with sustained benefits.

Event Decisions:

Weigh costs and rewards when confronting sudden events; different choices will lead to different situations.

Project Advancement:

Invest resources toward stage objectives, complete grand constructions, and build an absolute advantage for the mid-to-late game.

Advisor Combinations:

Combine advisor abilities with reputation growth to form distinct operational playstyles.

Game Features:
• Start Anytime, No Dragging｜~10 minutes per session, tight pacing—enjoy the thrill of deep strategy even in fragmented time.
• Chain Reactions, Every Step Counts｜Deep resource conversion choices reshape your territory’s development in real time with every move.
• Precise Interlocking, Maximum Strategy｜Edicts, events, projects, and advisor systems are tightly interconnected, offering a clearly tangible strategic space and rejecting mindless numerical steamrolling.
