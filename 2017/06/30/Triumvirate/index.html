Triumvirate

Editor Review

Go to AppStore

Brief

Material design collisions with match three.

The integration of quests and skills.

Simple and fun elimination.


The Triumvirate is a simple abstract elimination game, the extreme kill time artifact. Your goal is to eliminate more scores before the turns is used out. Each scene has the same goal, but different challenge. Be careful of the danger of the scene, or you can control it to complete the target.


-Complete the specified elimination task to get premium turns

-Flexible use of unique skill bundles to achieve higher scores

-Challenge to unlock more powerful skills in specific scenes

-Save and Sync in iCloud, never worry about losing your level
